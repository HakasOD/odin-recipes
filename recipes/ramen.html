How to make ramen!

Description

Eating ramen is certainally a joy. But outside of Japan it is hard to get that authentic taste. But this ramen achives what you've been dying for since your last trip to Japan. Dare I say it is ever better than the real thing.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sweetcorn
  • 50g cabbage
  • 60g bean sprouts
  • 15g fried onion
  • 1 soy marinated egg
  • 35g roasted sliced pork
  • 100g ramen noodles
  • 30ml ramen noodle soup base
  • 270ml water

Steps

  1. For the marinated eggs, bring the ingredients for A to the boil (except the eggs). Remove from heat then place in the peeled boiled eggs and marinate for 3 hours.
  2. For marinated pork, boil the pork for 30 minutes. Mix together the remaining ingredients for B. Place this and the cooked pork in a pressure cooker and cook for 20 minutes.
  3. Boil the cabbage and bean sprouts for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Prepare the noodles according to the packet instructions.
  5. Bring approx. 540 ml water to the boil in a pot, mix with 60ml Kikkoman Ramen Soup Base.
  6. Divide the soup into 2 bowls. Drain the noodles well and add to the soup.
  7. Halve eggs and slice the cooked pork.
  8. Place the eggs, sliced pork, cabbage, bean sprouts and sweetcorn into the soup. Sprinkle with fried onions.
